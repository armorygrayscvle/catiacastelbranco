About

Behind the lens

I'm Catia Castelbranco — a photographer based in Lisbon, Portugal, with a passion for quiet moments and honest light.

My work spans portrait, editorial, wedding and landscape photography. Every session begins with listening — understanding the people, the place, and the feeling that needs to be preserved. The result is imagery that is both timeless and deeply personal.

With over a decade of experience, I have had the privilege of collaborating with individuals, couples, and brands across Europe. Whether you are planning a wedding, a campaign, or simply want photographs that truly look like you — I would love to hear from you.
